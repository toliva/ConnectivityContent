Frequently Asked Questions

Arriving, Sleeping, Eating

  • Where is my hotel confirmation? Hotel confirmations will be emailed to you directly closer to the event. If you received a confirmation email from us on May 20 then you are definitely registered. If you did not receive a confirmation email, please contact your account manager or inquire in the Hackathon HipChat room.
  • Are meals provided? More information about meals is on the Hackathon Page. If you have dietary restrictions, please work with your account manager to get in contact with the hackathon organizing team and we will do what we can.
  • Airport Transfer There are several ways you can get from the Airport to the hotel:
    • Book a shuttle from the Airport here on Expedia.
    • The Prague Airport Official Transport Page has useful information, including a bus/train map.
    • Take a taxi directly from the airport

Hackathon Event

  • How much time will we have? You will have the time between when we start the hackathon till when the judging begins. Some teams will work late into the night to get as far as they can before the judging, but this is not required.
  • Can we prepare? YES! There are many things you can do to prepare. Here are some ideas:
    • Refine / clarify the problem you are trying to solve
    • Read up on the APIs you plan to use
    • Write and test sample code that calls the APIs you plan to use
    • Ask your Expedia Hackathon coach for suggestions
    While you won't use your sample code in your hackathon project, the experience you gain through the above efforts will help you make more effective use of your time during the hackathon. You are expected to write the code for your project during the hackathon.

Questions of Other Categories

  • Visa Invitation Letter If you require an invitation letter to apply for a visa, please contact your account manager and we will work with them to get you an invitation letter.
